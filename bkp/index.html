About Tukaram Kanthale

A Visionary Leader for the Community

Tukaram Kanthale is a dynamic leader and social worker with a proven track record of improving lives in Kharghar. With unwavering dedication, he has championed transparency, education, and social welfare, inspiring countless individuals to join his vision for a better tomorrow.

From advocating for an alcohol-free Kharghar to addressing critical community issues, Tukaram’s leadership is marked by action and compassion. His journey is a testament to the power of dedication and the impact of a committed leader.
